About us

Pharma Complex Solutions was founded in 2013. Our team consists of specialists in the field of quality control, validation, audit and pharmaceutical logistics, clinical trials logistics services and distribution with nearly 20 years of professional experience in the pharmaceutical industry in the areas of manufacturing and wholesale trade.

PCS customers include both local pharmaceutical companies as well as global concerns, including the US market. The company cooperates with manufactures of medicinal products and medical devices, pharmaceutical wholesalers, CRO companies and logistics operators servicing the pharmaceutical market. The company operates in Poland, Ukraine and Georgia.

The pharmaceutical industry appreciated our reliability by adopting PCS in 2015 to the membership of the prestigious Chamber of Commerce, FARMACJA POLSKA. It associates a large part of domestic and foreign companies from the pharmaceutical sector.
